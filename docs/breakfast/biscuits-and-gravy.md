### Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
3. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
4. Gradually add the milk, stirring until the dough comes together.
5. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead gently.
6. Roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thickness and cut into biscuits using a round cutter.
7. Place the biscuits on a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
8. While the biscuits are baking, prepare the gravy by cooking sausage in a skillet until browned
9. Sprinkle flour over the cooked sausage and stir to combine.
10. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until the gravy thickens.
11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
12. Serve the biscuits warm, topped with the sausage gravy.

### Notes:
- For a spicier gravy, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes
- You can also add shredded cheese to the biscuits for extra flavor
- Leftover biscuits can be reheated in the oven or toaster oven for a few minutes
- This dish pairs well with a side of scrambled eggs or fresh fruit
- Enjoy your homemade biscuits and gravy!
