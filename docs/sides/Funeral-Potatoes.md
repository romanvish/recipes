# Funeral Potatoes
## Added By: Lisa Lybbert
### Attributed to: https://tastesbetterfromscratch.com/funeral-potatoes

### Preparation Time: 20 minutes
### Cooking Time: 40-50 minutes

### Ingredients List:
- ▢ 30 ounces frozen hash browns, diced or shredded (THAWED*)
- ▢ 2 cups sour cream
- ▢ 10.5 ounce can cream of chicken soup (or homemade)
- ▢ 10 Tablespoons butter, divided, melted
- ▢ 1 teaspoon salt
- ▢ 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ▢ 1 teaspoon dried minced onion
- ▢ 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- ▢ 2 cups corn flakes cereal

### Instructions:
1. Allow potatoes to thaw in your fridge overnight, or spread them on a baking sheet and warm them in the oven at 200°F for about 20 minutes, until thawed.
2. Preheat oven to 350°F.
3. Combine sour cream, cream of chicken soup, 6 Tablespoons of melted butter, salt, pepper, and dried onion in a bowl. Mix well.
4. Add potatoes and shredded cheese and stir to combine. Spoon mixture into a single layer in a 9x13'' pan.
5. Add cornflakes to a large ziplock bag and crush gently with your hands or a rolling pin.
6. Add remaining 4 tablespoons of melted butter to the crushed cornflakes and combine well. Sprinkle mixture over potatoes.
7. Bake uncovered at 350°F for 40-50 minutes.
8. Serve these with baked ham, oven-roasted turkey, or flank steak.

### Notes:
- *Or substitute with 10 small potatoes, parboiled and diced.
- **To Freeze**: Make as directed, but do not add the cornflake topping. Cover and store in the freezer for up to 3 months. When ready to bake, thaw in the fridge overnight. Add cornflake topping before baking.
